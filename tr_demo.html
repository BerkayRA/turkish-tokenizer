Turkish Morphological Tokenizer

Decomposes inflected Turkish words into their roots and morphemes. Recognizes ~50 suffixes covering nominal and verbal inflection, mood, voice, derivation, participles, and compound tenses.

Options

Diacritics are matched automatically — mekân = mekan, resmî = resmi.
